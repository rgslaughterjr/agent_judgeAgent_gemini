INTERNAL AI USAGE POLICY - CONFIDENTIAL
Effective Date: January 15, 2024
Version: 2.1

SECTION 1: ACCEPTABLE USE
All employees must ensure that AI tools are used responsibly.
- Do not input PII (Personally Identifiable Information) into public LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude) unless an Enterprise agreement is in place.
- All AI-generated code must be reviewed by a human senior engineer before merging to main.

SECTION 2: PROHIBITED MODELS
The following models are strictly prohibited for use with internal data due to data retention policies:
- Model-X (Public Free Tier)
- Any model hosted on servers outside of approved regions (US-East, EU-West).

SECTION 3: DATA CLASSIFICATION
- "Confidential" data may only be processed by self-hosted models or approved VPC endpoints.
- "Public" data may be processed by standard API endpoints.

SECTION 4: AGENT REGISTRATION
All autonomous agents must be registered in the 'Agent Registry' before deployment.
Each agent must have a designated human owner responsible for its actions.
