# NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) 1.0

## GOVERN

**GOVERN 1**: Policies, processes, procedures, and practices across the organization related to the mapping, measuring, and managing of AI risks are in place, transparent, and implemented effectively.

### GOVERN 1.1

**Description**: Legal and regulatory requirements involving AI are understood, managed, and documented.
**Implementation**: Organizations should maintain a legal registry of all AI-related laws (e.g., EU AI Act, GDPR, CCPA) and ensure all AI systems are assessed against them prior to deployment.

### GOVERN 1.2

**Description**: The characteristics of trustworthy AI are integrated into organizational policies, processes, procedures, and practices.
**Implementation**: Trustworthy characteristics include Valid & Reliable, Safe, Secure & Resilient, Accountable & Transparent, Explainable & Interpretable, Privacy-Enhanced, and Fair.

## MAP

**MAP 1**: Context is established and risks related to the AI system are identified and enhanced.

### MAP 1.1

**Description**: Context is established and understood.
**Implementation**: The specific setting in which the AI system will be deployed is analyzed. This includes the socio-technical environment and potential downstream impacts.
